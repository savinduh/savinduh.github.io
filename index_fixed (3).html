About Me...

I'm Savindu Herath — a Graphic Designer and UI/UX Designer based in Sri Lanka, with 3+ years of freelance experience building visual identities, social campaigns, and digital interfaces for clients across branding, education, and competition spaces.

I work across Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Figma to take a brand from concept to execution. I've helped clients grow social engagement by up to 40% through consistent, story-driven design systems — and I care as much about strategy as I do about craft.

I'm currently open to remote mid-level design roles — graphic design, UI/UX, or visual design — where I can bring that same client-focused thinking to a team working on something that matters.
